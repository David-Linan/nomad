◆ ComputeType

enum ComputeType
strong
Enumerator
STANDARD 

f is OBJ. h is the squared sum of violations of all constraints. EB constraint violation result in h being INF.

PHASE_ONE 

f is computed based on the violation of EB constraints only. h is always 0. OBJ and PB constraints are ignored.

USER 

f and h are computed by a user-provided function.

UNDEFINED 

Undefined: This value may be used when the ComputeType is not mandatory
